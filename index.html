Biography

I am JinKe Xiong. This website is organized in the style of an academic homepage and serves as a central place for my profile, research-facing interests, code repositories, and future academic updates.

I use this page to present contact information, research topics, selected technical work, and project links in a format that is easy to browse for collaborators, advisors, and peers.

Research

Research Interests

Accelerator Physics

Topics around beam dynamics, accelerator-related modeling, and scientific computation for charged particle systems.

Electron Beam Simulation

Numerical studies of density-modulated or microbunched electron beams and their evolution in drift sections.

Scientific Computing

Computational workflows, reproducible simulation pipelines, and research software used to support academic work.
